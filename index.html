Our Academic Levels

We follow the Ugandan National Curriculum, focusing on academic excellence, talent development, and character building.

Nursery Section (Pre-Primary)

Baby Class

Ages 3-4. Introduction to socialization, basic sounds, and motor skills through play.

Middle Class

Ages 4-5. Developing language proficiency, number recognition, and creative arts.

Top Class

Ages 5-6. Preparing for Primary transition with reading, writing, and arithmetic basics.

Primary Section (P.1 - P.7)

Lower Primary
P.1 - P.3

Thematic curriculum focusing on literacy and numeracy foundations.

Transition Stage
P.4

Transitioning from local language to English as the medium of instruction.

Upper Primary
P.5 - P.6

In-depth study of SST, Science, Math, and English Language.

Candidate Class
P.7 (PLE)

Intensive preparation for Primary Leaving Examinations (PLE).
